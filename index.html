oai-style-cover, an editorial cover generator

Nine cover styles drawn in the browser with Canvas 2D: Cool Optical, Organic Bloom, Aurora Film, Minimal Type, Utility Cards, Brand Lockup, Product UI, Semantic Object and Signal Poster. Pick one, type a title, choose a 1:1, 16:9 or 4:5 frame, and save a PNG up to 5120 px for a blog post, a deck or a social card. You can also compose your own screenshot or logo into the cover. Nothing is uploaded and no image model is involved: every frame is a program, and the same seed always draws the same cover.
